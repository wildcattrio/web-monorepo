FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is my data private?

Yes. Your data stays on your device and in your private backups. We never sell or share personal information.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes. Elevate Quotes includes an offline database so you can browse and create quote art without internet access.

How do I upgrade or cancel Pro?

Manage your subscription through the App Store or Google Play. You can upgrade, cancel, or switch plans at any time from the store settings.

Which devices are supported?

Elevate Quotes works on modern iOS and Android phones and tablets. We recommend iOS 15+ or Android 10+ for the best experience.

How do notifications work?

You can enable hourly reminders in the app. We schedule smart delivery windows so you're not disturbed at night and can adjust frequency at any time in Settings.

Can I export/share quote art without watermark?

Yes. Pro plans let you export high‑resolution images without watermark and share directly to social platforms or save to your gallery.
