FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How accurate is CalSmart AI?

Our AI uses Google's Gemini to identify foods and estimate portions. For packaged foods, we pull exact nutrition data from FatSecret's database of millions of products. While AI estimates are helpful for quick logging, you can always adjust portions manually for maximum accuracy.

Does CalSmart AI integrate with other health apps?

Yes! On Android, CalSmart AI integrates with Health Connect to sync data from Fitbit, Samsung Health, Oura Ring, and other compatible apps. On iOS, we support Apple Health integration. This lets you automatically import steps and exercise data.

Can I use CalSmart AI offline?

CalSmart AI works offline for viewing your logs, saved foods, and dashboard. When you're offline, any new entries are saved locally and automatically synced when you're back online. AI photo recognition requires an internet connection.

Is CalSmart AI free?

CalSmart AI is free to download and use with all core features included. We may introduce optional premium features in the future, but the essential calorie tracking experience will always be free.

What AI does CalSmart use?

CalSmart AI uses Google's Gemini AI for food recognition, voice logging, recipe generation, and exercise logging. Your photos are processed securely and are not stored. Nutrition data for packaged foods comes from FatSecret's comprehensive database.
