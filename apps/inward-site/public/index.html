FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How does the memory system work?

When you write entries, Inward's AI builds a memory of the people, places, events, and patterns in your life. When generating reflections or chatting, it draws on this memory to give you deeply personal responses. The more you write, the better it knows you.

Is my journal data private?

Yes. All your journal data stays on your device. Entries are sent to Google's Gemini AI for reflections, but Google does not store your data. There are no user accounts — just an optional local PIN. We never sell your data to third parties.

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

Free gives you 3 entries per day with basic AI reflections (no memory). Pro unlocks unlimited entries, the full memory system, AI chat, pattern insights, and advanced analytics. Pro includes a 7-day free trial.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel anytime through your App Store or Google Play subscription settings. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your billing period. Your journal entries are never deleted — they stay on your device.

What AI does Inward use?

Inward uses Google's Gemini AI for generating reflections and powering the chat feature. Your entries are sent to Gemini for processing but are not stored by Google. The memory system is built and stored locally on your device.

Is Inward available on both iOS and Android?

Yes! Inward is available on both the App Store (iOS) and Google Play (Android). The experience is the same on both platforms.
