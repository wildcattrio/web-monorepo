Our Story

WildCat Trio Limited is a registered Irish software company building consumer mobile applications. We're focused on creating high-quality, privacy-first apps that use AI to simplify everyday tasks.

We're a small, early-stage startup — bootstrapped, independent, and shipping real products to real users on the Google Play Store. Our flagship product, CalSmart AI, uses Google Gemini AI as its core intelligence, with Firebase and Google Cloud powering the entire backend.

We believe AI should make everyday tasks easier, not more complicated. That's why we focus on practical applications — food tracking you can do with a photo, journaling that actually understands you, and inspiration delivered on your schedule.
