How We Build

Small team.
High standards.

🔒

Privacy First

Your data stays on your device. We don't sell it, share it, or build ad profiles from it. That's a promise, not a policy.

✨

Craft Over Speed

We'd rather ship one great feature than five mediocre ones. Every screen, every animation, every interaction is deliberate.

🎯

Do One Thing Well

Origin Story

Named after
three cats.

WildCat Trio wasn't born in a startup accelerator or a Silicon Valley garage. It started at a kitchen table in Ireland, with a laptop, an idea, and three very opinionated cats watching from the sofa.

Santiago. Satoshi. Salem. The company is named after them — because they were there for every late-night debugging session, every "why won't this build," every breakthrough moment.

The goal has always been simple: build apps that make daily life a little better. Apps that are genuinely useful, beautifully designed, and that respect the people using them. No VC pressure. No growth-at-all-costs. Just good software, made with care.

The Builder

William Meyler

"I'm not a trained developer. I'm someone who kept building things until they worked — and then kept making them better."

William is a self-taught builder from Ireland who learned to code by shipping real products. No bootcamp. No CS degree. Just curiosity, persistence, and three cats providing moral support.

He calls himself a "vibe coder" — someone who learns by doing, experiments constantly, and builds things that feel right. WildCat Trio is his studio, his lab, and his life's work in progress.
